Quest DSilHand_M30ScholarFragment

This quest is given to the player by Krev The Skinner after he completes the second quest. He will ask you to meet Fjol at Driftshade Refugee. Fjol will send you to Winterhold and talk to Nelacar. Nelacar will give you a letter with the results of his investigations, telling the mages of are studying a fragment of Wuuthrad. The player's mission will be to enter the college and retrieve it. Once the player retrieves the fragment, you will be able to deliver it to Fjol and complete the quest.
