Quest DSilHand_S02IconoclasticRevenge

In this side quest Fjol reveal to you he knows the location of the Totems of Hircine in Skyrim and wants your help to banish the items. In this quest, the player has to find the location of all the items and banish them by burning the totems in the Eternal Flame in the Ysgramour tomb.
